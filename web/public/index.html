What this is

A TRON-styled terminal emulator. Two ways to run it — each with a different scope.

Web this page

  • Can — in-browser sandbox shell (no install)
  • Can — connect to your own backend once installed
  • Can't — touch your computer without the installer
  • Can't — work on a phone (needs real keyboard)

Desktop app full

  • Can — spawn real shells on your OS
  • Can — pair your phone via QR + tunnel
  • Can — live system widgets (CPU, memory, net, geo)
  • Can't — run without download + install

Get started

  1. Just try it — close this and you land in the browser sandbox. No install.
  2. Want real shells? Paste this into Terminal on your Mac or Linux machine: 
    curl -fsSL https://www.s0a.app/install.sh | sh
  3. Reload. The site auto-detects your local backend and unlocks full mode.
