This is a full terminal emulator. It needs a real keyboard and the layout doesn't collapse to a phone screen. Open on a laptop or desktop browser to use it.

To set it up on your computer:

  1. Open www.s0a.app on your Mac or Linux machine.
  2. Paste this into a terminal once: 
    curl -fsSL https://www.s0a.app/install.sh | sh
  3. Reload the page — you'll land in a real shell.

Or pair your phone as a secondary view: install on your computer first, then use the MOBILE LINK QR from the desktop sidebar.
